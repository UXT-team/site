SHORT CODE

A short code is a “5 or 6” digit number that can send and receive SMS (and MMS) to and from mobile phones. It can be easily memorized by your customers, suppliers or employees, making it simple for them to respond to your messages.

Short codes come in two varieties: vanity and random. A vanity short code is a specific number that you choose, while a random short code is assigned to you and can’t be reviewed in advance.

Our short Code are critical because they will help:

  • • Generate new leads to build your database.
  • • Customer loyalty programs and rewards.
  • • Targeted, location-based notifications.
  • • Send coupons, exclusive discounts or offers Manage bookings or appointments.
  • • Send time-sensitive delivery alerts.
  • • Provide progress reports on orders or production.
  • • Get customer feedback with SMS survey.
