PF Manager Mobile App

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

PF Manager is a complete personal finance manager for managing your finances. It enables you track your expenditures and income, manage your budgets, analyze your personal finance records, compare your income to expenditures and compare your expenses with your budget and generally keep track of your finances in a simple way. It also provides frequently updated money saving tips and investment ideas in the tips section. This application also enables you to contact a personal finance consultant. PF Manager has secure online backup for your personal finance records which enable you to retain your data when you lose or change your phone.

 


Features.
- Record expenses
- Record income
- Analyze income and expenditures
- Budget and compare budget to expenses
- Financial investment and money saving tips
- Professional personal finance consultant
- Cashflow reports
- Customizable currency
